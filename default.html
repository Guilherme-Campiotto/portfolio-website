About me

A game developer of indie games for Steam. I have developed and published premium games on Steam, a free-to-play game on mobile for android and made several free games/prototypes for PC to test and learn different ideas. I love to try new things, so I created games of different genres like arcade, puzzle, platform and tower defense.

Four years of experience working on IT as a full stack developer and two years working with Unity, with skills in Java, C# and Javascript. Graduated in Game Development by FATEC.

The Final Wall

Previous Next
Language: Python
Download game
Github

A tower defense game for PC, a survivor must resist the zombie waves during the day, rebuild his defenses and search for weapons during the night. Inspired by the Last Stand. This project was made using the library Pygame.

Main features:

  • Ten different levels.
  • Four weapons with different status (damage, speed, ammo and more).
  • Three types of zombies, with different looks and attributes.
  • A day and night cycle. During day the player kill zombies that are trying to invade, by night he must distribute points that represent hours to fix defenses or search for new weapons.
  • A survivor diary, he tells his days and a new page is unlocked in each level.
